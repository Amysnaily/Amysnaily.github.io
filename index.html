З Given to the Gravelord Servant to rank up while in the Gravelord Servant covenant. While an Eye of Death is active, visible black energy will begin emanating from the player. When using Eye of Death (must be unhollowed) a summon sign will appear in the worlds of the three victims. The three victims may invade the world of the Gravelord Servant all at once. The host will receive one Eye of Death for each invader killed. Upon death of the Gravelord Servant, the message "Disasters are gone after the defeat of the Gravelord Servant" will appear for victims..
